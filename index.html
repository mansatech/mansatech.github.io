Focus Group: Small and Medium Enterprises

  • SMEs are a key part of Uganda's economy

    Small and Medium sized enterprises comprise up to 70% of Uganda’s economy and contribute to 20% of Gross Domestic Product. They also employ up to 45% of the country’s labour force.

  • The most succesful SMEs are able to self disrupt

    Small and Medium Enterprises that disrupt themselves are up to 30% more likely to make the jump into the large business category. This is most often by automating processes, use of industry standard enterprise resource planning tools, collecting customer and sales data and leveraging anayltics, deploying custom self service platforms for customer excellence, diversified payment options.
